# The Story of WikiPortraits

## How a Vision for Open Knowledge Became Reality

Welcome to the story of WikiPortraits – a project born from the belief that high-quality photography should be freely available to document the world's most notable people.

### The Beginning

It started with a simple observation: Wikipedia, the world's largest encyclopedia, often lacks professional portraits of contemporary figures. While historical figures have paintings and photographs in the public domain, modern personalities are often represented by low-quality snapshots or no images at all.

### The Mission

WikiPortraits aims to change this by:

- Creating professional, high-quality portraits of notable individuals
- Releasing all images under Creative Commons licenses
- Making these portraits freely available for educational and journalistic use
- Contributing to the global commons of human knowledge
